FAQ

Common Questions, Honest Answers

How long does it take to launch my OTT platform?
A standard platform covering mobile, web, and 2–3 TV platforms launches in 6–10 weeks. Larger enterprise builds with Samsung, LG, and custom features take 10–16 weeks. You'll get an exact, milestone-based timeline during your free discovery session — before any work begins.
Which platforms and devices do you build for?
All the major ones: iOS, Android, Apple TV, Android TV, Samsung Smart TV, LG Smart TV, Amazon Fire TV, Roku, and responsive web — plus Chromecast and AirPlay. We handle native development, certification, and app store submission for each.
Do I own the platform after it's built?
Completely. You own the code, the design, the subscriber data, and the app store accounts. We're your development partner, not a SaaS provider — no lock-in and no monthly platform fees.
How do you protect my content from piracy?
Every platform ships with Widevine, FairPlay, and PlayReady multi-DRM, AES-128 encryption, secure token authentication, and geo-blocking. Advanced plans add forensic watermarking — invisible, viewer-specific markers that trace any leak back to its source.
Is there a free consultation available?
Always — free and with zero obligation. Book a strategy session with our OTT specialists and receive a custom proposal, including a device roadmap, timeline, and transparent cost estimate, within 48 hours.
