Common questions
Got questions about OTT development? We have honest answers.
How long does it take to launch my OTT platform?
A standard multi-device platform covering mobile, web, and 2–3 TV platforms typically launches in 6–10 weeks. Enterprise builds with Samsung, LG, and custom features take 10–16 weeks. We give you an exact milestone-based timeline during the free discovery session — before any work begins.
Which platforms and devices do you build for?
We build for all major streaming platforms: iOS, Android, Apple TV (tvOS), Android TV, Samsung Smart TV (Tizen), LG Smart TV (webOS), Amazon Fire TV, Roku, and responsive web. We also support Chromecast and AirPlay. We handle native development, certification, and app store submission for every platform.
Do I own the platform after it is built?
Yes — completely. You own the codebase, the design, the subscriber data, and the app store accounts. We are a development and technology partner, not a SaaS platform. No lock-in, no monthly platform fees, no strings attached.
How do you protect my content from piracy?
Every platform includes Widevine (Android/Chrome), FairPlay (Apple), and PlayReady (Windows) multi-DRM, AES-128 content encryption, secure token authentication, and geo-blocking. Advanced plans add forensic watermarking — invisible viewer-specific identifiers that trace leaked content to its source.
Is there a free consultation available?
Yes — always free, zero obligation. Book a strategy session with our OTT specialists and receive a custom platform proposal — including device roadmap, timeline, and transparent cost estimate — within 48 hours.
