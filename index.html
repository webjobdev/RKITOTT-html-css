FAQ
Common Questions, Honest Answers
How long does it take to launch my OTT platform?
A standard platform covering mobile, web, and two to three TV platforms usually launches in 6–10 weeks. Larger enterprise builds with Samsung, LG, and custom features take 10–16 weeks. You'll get an exact, milestone-based timeline during your free discovery session — before any work begins.
Which platforms and devices do you build for?
All the major ones: iOS, Android, Apple TV, Android TV, Samsung Smart TV, LG Smart TV, Amazon Fire TV, Roku, and responsive web — plus Chromecast and AirPlay. We handle native development, certification, and app store submission for each.
Do I own the platform after it is built?
Completely. You own the code, the design, the subscriber data, and the app store accounts. We're your development partner, not a SaaS provider — no lock-in and no monthly platform fees.
How do you protect my content from piracy?
Every platform includes Widevine (Android/Chrome), FairPlay (Apple), and PlayReady (Windows) multi-DRM, AES-128 content encryption, secure token authentication, and geo-blocking. Advanced plans add forensic watermarking — invisible viewer-specific identifiers that trace leaked content to its source.
